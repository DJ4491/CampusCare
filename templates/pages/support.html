Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report an issue on campus?
Click on the "+" button in the navigation menu and select "Report Issue". Fill in the required details including the location, category, and description of the issue. You can also add an image to better illustrate the problem.
How can I track my reported issues?
Visit "My Reports" section in your profile to view all your submitted reports and their current status. You can also receive notifications about updates on your reports.
How do I update my profile information?
Click on your profile picture in the navigation bar and select "Edit Profile". Here you can update your personal information, profile picture, and notification preferences.
